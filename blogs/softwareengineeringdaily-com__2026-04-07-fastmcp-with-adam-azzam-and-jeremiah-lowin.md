<p style="text-align: justify;">The Model Context Protocol, or MCP, gives developers a common way to expose tools, data, and capabilities to large language models, and it has quickly become an important standard in agentic AI. FastMCP is an open source project stewarded by the team at <a href="https://www.prefect.io/">Prefect</a>, which is an orchestration platform for AI and data workflows. The FastMCP project builds on MCP to provide high-level, ergonomic abstractions for Python developers to rapidly build and deploy MCP servers and applications.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="https://x.com/jlowin">Jeremiah Lowin</a> is the founder and CEO of Prefect, and <a href="https://x.com/AAAzzam">Adam Azzam</a> is the VP of Product at the company. In this episode, Jeremiah and Adam join Gregor Vand to discuss the origin story of FastMCP, the three pillars of the framework, the architectural decisions behind FastMCP 3.0, and much more.</p>
